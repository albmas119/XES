About

Xceptional Events story began in 2012. Back then we were a small group of creative single parents who decided to earn an extra income, we pooled our ideas together and nine (9) years later, we are leaders in event management and are standing firm. Xceptional Events has two (2) offices, our main office is located in Port of Spain Trinidad and our second office is in Scarborough Tobago. In all our established years we have successfully managed private parties, weddings, charitable and corporate events etc. We have done work for Caribbean Airlines, Hyatt and our former president Ms. Paula Mae Weekes. We have also done work for Scotia Bank Jamaica, Massy Barbados, just to name a few. Our staffing consists of Permanent, temporary and voluntary staff. Our services include designing, coordinating, social media and much more. We are privileged to connect with millions of customers throughout Trinidad and Tobago and the Caribbean.
